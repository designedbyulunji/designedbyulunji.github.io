about us

Organizational background

Friends of Environmental (FoE) is registered Local Non-Governmental Organization under Companies Act (Cap 46:03), Laws of Malawi. An empowered community leading to informed decision on environmental management and sustainable development. The impact areas in which the organization implement its various projects are Rumphi district and Mzimba North (Malawi). These areas are among the vulnerable areas with high environmental degradation due to land settlements, agricultural activities, land ownership and high deforestation rate for energy purposes. Therefore, the areas are prone to disasters.

Mission

To empower community institutions particularly women, the youth, the orphans the landless and the homeless on interventions of sustainable management of the environment through promotion of sustainable energy technologies, afforestation, education, access to safe water, improved hygiene, climate change adaptation measures, sanitation, access to land and decent housing in order to improve the environmental quality of our community, reduce poverty levels, eradicate hunger and improve health.

Organizational goal

The goal of the Organization is to empower communities to take a leading role in management of environment in line with Government policies in order to contribute to the achievement of the Malawi Growth and Development Strategy (MDGS), Malawi 2063 and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs 1,2,4, 6, 7 & 13) in Malawi.

Main Objective

The main objective of the Organization is to work with communities for sustainable utilization of natural resources, reducing the rate of environmental degradation and help in mitigating the effects of climate change and provide climate change adaptation measures to reduce the burden of ill health, poverty, landlessness, homelessness through community-based approaches.
